About Mechatronic

Innovating Engineering Solutions, Delivering Excellence

Established in 2007, Mechatronic Systems has become a trusted name in the field of manufacturing and engineering solutions. With over 30 years of expertise, our CEO, Mr. Vishwas V. Kulkarni, leads the company with a focus on delivering innovative and customized solutions to meet the specific needs of our clients.

We specialize in the design and development of Special Purpose Machines (SPMs), catering to industries across India, the Gulf, East Asia, and Europe. Our products are known for their reliability, quality, and cost-effectiveness—offering better performance than Chinese competitors and affordability compared to European manufacturers.
