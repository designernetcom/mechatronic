Welding Automation

Description

Welding automation systems are advanced solutions designed to improve welding efficiency, precision, and productivity. These systems reduce labor costs, minimize errors, and ensure consistent quality for a wide range of welding applications. Ideal for automotive, aerospace, construction, and other heavy industries.

Specifications Description
Type of welding MIG or TIG with filler
Component Material MS, SS, Aluminium
Type of operation Linear or Rotary
Gantry Linear x, y, z axis & rotary
Control System PLC with HMI, Servo operated
Speed of Welding & Dry-run 500 mm/min for welding. 10000 mm/min for dry-run
  • Automotive manufacturing and component welding.
  • Construction and heavy equipment fabrication.
  • Aerospace industry welding tasks.
  • Shipbuilding and marine equipment welding.
  • Energy and power plant equipment fabrication.
